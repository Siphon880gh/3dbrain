
## Add
Make a cube, plane, sphere, circle, cone, torus, etc:
Shift+A → Mesh → Cube/etc
G → drag into place

^ They'll be adde wherever your 3D cursor is at (SHIFT+Right click to set 3D cursor coordinates)
